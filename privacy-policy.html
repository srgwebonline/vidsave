1. Overview & Commitment to Privacy

At Shorts Creator Toolkit ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices in compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations.

Our Privacy-First Approach

No Account Required: Use our tools without creating an account

Client-Side Processing: Most tools run entirely in your browser

No Data Storage: We don't store your YouTube/Instagram URLs or generated content

No Tracking: We don't use invasive tracking cookies or sell your data

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Tool Inputs: URLs, keywords, and settings you enter into our tools
  • Contact Information: Only if you voluntarily contact us via email

2.2 Automated Collection

  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, IP address (anonymized)
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, tools used, session duration
  • Cookies: Only essential and functional cookies (see Section 5)

2.3 What We DON'T Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • YouTube/Google account credentials
  • Instagram/Facebook login information
  • Payment information (our service is free)
  • Sensitive personal data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide analytics and insights
  • Improve tool accuracy and performance

3.2 Analytics & Improvements
