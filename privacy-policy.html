1. Introduction

Welcome to Shorts Creator Toolkit ("we," "our," or "us"). We operate the website shortscreator.tools (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our free YouTube Shorts and Instagram Reels creation tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be perfectly clear about what we do not collect:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No Video Content: We do not access, store, or process the actual content of your YouTube Shorts or Instagram Reels.
  • No Account Data: We do not require accounts and therefore collect no login credentials or profile information.
  • No Financial Data: All our tools are completely free with no payment processing.
  • No Location Data: We do not track or store your geographical location.

3. Client-Side Processing

All our tools are designed to run entirely in your web browser (client-side). This means:

  • URLs you enter for thumbnail extraction or analysis are processed locally in your browser
  • Hashtag generation and caption creation happen on your device
  • No data is transmitted to our servers for processing
  • Your inputs never leave your device unless you explicitly share them

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal browser storage for essential functionality:

  • Theme Preference: We store your dark/light mode preference in local storage
  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies for basic site functionality
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or any tracking cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect theme persistence.

5. Third-Party Services

YouTube API Services

When you use our thumbnail downloader or info viewer, your browser directly accesses YouTube's public thumbnail servers. We do not intermediate this connection.

Google AdSense

We display Google AdSense ads to support the free operation of our tools. AdSense may use cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ad Settings.

Fonts and Icons

We use Google Fonts and FontAwesome for typography and icons. These services may collect limited usage data as per their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Security

While we don't collect personal data, we implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Secure coding practices
  • Regular security audits
  • No database storage of user information

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Legal Compliance

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR principles even though we don't collect personal data. You have the right to:

  • Access any data we might hold about you (none)
  • Request deletion of data (not applicable)
  • Object to processing (not applicable)
  • Data portability (not applicable)

CCPA Compliance

For California residents, we comply with CCPA requirements. Since we don't collect personal information, we don't sell or share your data.
